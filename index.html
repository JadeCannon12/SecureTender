How It Works

Every bid follows a clear, calm process — from first idea to final submission.

  1. 1. Discovery: We talk through the opportunity, timeline and goals.
  2. 2. Fit Check: We assess eligibility, score potential and resource capacity.
  3. 3. Storyboarding: We map out win themes and key answers before writing.
  4. 4. Draft & Review: You review versions as we refine for clarity and compliance.
  5. 5. Submit & Debrief: We handle the upload and share lessons for next time.

About

SecureTender exists to make tendering achievable for UK SMEs — cutting through jargon, simplifying process, and helping great businesses win the work they deserve. The approach is calm, practical, and rooted in real delivery — combining structured methods with plain-English communication that works in both regulated and fast-moving environments.

  • Supporting SMEs to compete confidently in public and private sector bids
  • Expertise across manufacturing, pharma, technology and service industries
  • Built on principles of clarity, credibility, and sustainable growth
  • Based in the North West · Remote-first · Supporting clients UK-wide
Do you write and submit, or just write?
Both. We can handle full portal submission end-to-end or deliver final responses for your team to upload.
Can you help with Social Value?
Yes — we build scored Social Value models with a practical delivery plan, costed activities, and measurable KPIs.
What if we’ve left it late?
We offer a 24–48h triage. If the risk is too high to submit a quality response, we’ll advise a fallback that protects future bids.
Do you work success-only?
Not solely. We can add a small success-fee element to a reduced fixed fee where appropriate.
